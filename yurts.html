Experience the unique charm of our yurts

Our yurts offer a blend of rustic charm and modern comfort, providing a unique lodging experience. Each yurt is equipped with cozy furnishings, heating, and stunning views of the surrounding nature. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a family adventure, our yurts are the perfect choice for your stay at Pacific Trails Resort.

What do yurts look like?

Yurts are traditional Mongolian tents that have been adapted for modern use. They are typically made of wood and canvas, and feature a distinctive dome shape.
